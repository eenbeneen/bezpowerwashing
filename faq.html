Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? You're not the only one!
Here are some of our most common questions.

Most Popular Questions

Q: What services do you offer?

A: We offer residential and commercial services, including pressure washing, soft washing, window cleaning and gutter cleaning.

Q: How much do your services cost?

A: The price of your service can differ based on the size and type of the areas that need to be cleaned. We offer free estimates to provide personal and accurate quotes.

Before Service

Q: How soon can I get an appointment scheduled?

A: We can schedule you within the week.

Q: How do I prepare for an appointment?

A: To prepare your property for a job, please remove chairs, tables and any other objects that could interfere with the cleaning.

During Service

Q: How long does a job take?

A: For our main services, we estimate that:

  • Pressure washing a home takes about 2 hours and 30 min.
  • Concrete pressure washing takes about 1 hour and 45 min.
  • Window cleaning normally takes about 1 hour and 30 min, but can take longer if you need to have a lot of windows cleaned.

Please note that these times are just estimates based on previous jobs.

Q: Should I be home during the job?

A: You do need to be home for the payment, but you don't have to be home the entire time.

After Service

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

A: Cash, check, or Zelle.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

A: We understand that we're not always perfect. If you have an issue with any part of the job, please contact us and we will come back and fix up any errors.
